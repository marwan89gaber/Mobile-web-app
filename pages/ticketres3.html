Great Hall

The Great Hall, the main auditorium, in a theater-style setting, can comfortably accommodate 1638 persons. It is fully air-conditioned and is suitable for international conferences, symposia, meetings, seminars, concerts, presentations and performances. The Hall is divided into four levels with eight exits, four on each side. The size of the stage is 20 x 21 meters. Behind the stage are ten backstage dressing rooms for the performers. The Great Hall is equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual devices providing services such as video conferencing, film presentations, and a cinema screen.
